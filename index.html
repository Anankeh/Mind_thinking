Learning architecture model

The architecture is a living system.

Outward pulse means governance, standard, or decision.
Inward pulse means evidence from real practice.
Rings follow NIST logic: Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover.
The moving point is the architect crossing every layer.
